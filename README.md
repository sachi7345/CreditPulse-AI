# CreditPulse AI

## The AI Credit Copilot for MSME Lending

> Accelerating Financial Inclusion Through Alternative Data, Explainable AI & Intelligent Credit Decisioning.

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional MSME lending depends on limited financial history and fragmented data sources, making it difficult for banks to accurately assess New-to-Credit (NTC) and New-to-Bank (NTB) enterprises.

---

## Solution

CreditPulse AI is an AI-powered MSME Financial Health Card platform that combines alternative financial data such as:

- GST
- UPI
- Account Aggregator
- EPFO
- Bank Statements
- Invoices

to generate:

- Financial Health Score
- Credit Risk Assessment
- Cash Flow Insights
- Loan Eligibility
- Explainable AI Report
- AI Recommendations
